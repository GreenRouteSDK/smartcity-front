Green Route: An app for pollution and traffic monitoring

The objective of Green Route is to help the final user to determine the best route to follow to reach a destination, taking into account the user profile (such as health conditions), and the user preferences, such as transport type. Green Route proposes the ideal route for the user, avoiding routes with high levels of pollution, floods or pollen, etc., allowing for instance, to obtain the preferred routes for people with respiratory diseases.
